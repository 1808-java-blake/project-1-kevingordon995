Welcome to CompanyCo. Reimbursement

CompanyCo. Reimbursement is here to help employees and finance managers assess and complete their reimbursement needs. Below are the services provided in order for employees and managers to correctly make decisions on reimbursement matters.

Services made available below and above in the navigation bar.

View Reimbursement Table

View current and past reimbursement tickets. Employees may look at their own ticket history while finance managers can view entire staff ticket history.

View Table »

Create New Reimbursement Ticket Request

Employees can navigate to this tab to create a new ticket for reimbursement. Reimbursement are categorized into four groups; Lodging, Travel, Food, and Other.

Create New Ticket »

Approve/Deny Reimbursement Ticket

Finance Managers navigate here in order to approve and/or deny reimbursement ticket requests. Employees who are not finance managers are not authorized to make ticket requests and will be denied the ability to do so.

Approve/Deny Ticket »
